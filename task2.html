Eiffel Tower
A visit to the Eiffel Tower is worth it. The monument offers spectacular views of the City of Lights, a world-class fine dining experience, and a chance to marvel at this architectural wonder.the Eiffel Tower has been a powerful and distinctive symbol of the city of Paris, and by extension, of France. At first, when it was built for the 1889 World's Fair, it impressed the entire world by its stature and daring design, and symbolized French know-how and industrial genius...

Great Wall of China
Mutianyu:a picturesque section of the wall that's well-preserved and less crowded than other parts. Badaling:a restored section of the wall that was built in the Qin Dynasty and is located in a natural pass. Simatai:a reconstructed village with a gondola lift that provides access to a steep and authentic part of the wall.Jinshanling Great Wall:a beautiful section of the Great Wall with 67 watchtowers along 20 kilometers of the wall. It's a popular place for sunset picnics and photography...

Navy-Pier
The Navy Pier is one of the most visited landmarks on Chicago's lakefront for visitors and locals. Originally known as Municipal Pier, it was designed as a hub for freight boats, passenger vehicles, and an array of events, including expos, conferences, and pageants. Navy Pier is an amusement park with rides and games, cultural attractions, restaurants, an exciting variety of parks, shopping options, walkable gardens, and more that line the pier...
